05. FEATURED PRODUCT

LoanTrack helps teams stay on top of compliance without living in spreadsheets.

AVAILABLE FOR LICENSING

LoanTrack v4.0

The compliance management system for Zambia's regulated lenders

It was built for lenders that need a proper system for borrower records, repayments, compliance evidence, and reporting. Everything sits in one place, so the team is not bouncing between spreadsheets and separate tools.

BoZ Compliance Ready

Automated monthly returns, KYC verification, audit trails, and a document vault, all in one system.

Complete Loan Lifecycle

From borrower onboarding to final repayment, LoanTrack keeps the full process in one place. You can track payments, generate loan agreements, and follow the portfolio as it changes.

Secure & Auditable

Role-based access control, auto-logout on inactivity, and a full audit log for every action in the system.

Built around the BFSA No. 9 of 2026

These are the main requirements from Zambia's new financial services law that it covers:

KYC & Borrower Verification
AML/CFT Suspicious Transaction Flagging
Monthly BoZ Return Generation (PDF)
Loan Cost-of-Credit Disclosure Documents
Compliance Dashboard & Readiness Score
Operational & IT Audit Trail
Document Vault for Policy Manuals
Data Protection Act Consent Logging
Interest Rate Compliance Monitoring
Role-Based Staff Access Control
